How to Buy

Create a Wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice!

Get Some ETH

have ETH in your wallet to switch to $PAPE. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Presale Box

Navigate to the dedicated Presale Box on our website where the token sale is taking place, and connect your wallet.

Switch ETH for $PAPE

In this step, you'll swap your ETH for $PAPE tokens. Just enter how much ETH you want to swap, and hit the "SWAP" button. A pop-up will appear asking you to confirm the transaction. Make sure to confirm it. Once you do, you'll see your $PAPE tokens in your dashboard. They'll be there waiting for you to claim them after the presale is over.
